## Challenges I Overcame

### 1. **Real-Time Synchronization**
Learning to manage state across multiple connected clients was challenging. I had to deeply understand event-driven architecture and handle edge cases like disconnections and race conditions.

### 2. **Deployment Complexity**
Moving from local development to production taught me about CORS, environment variables, database connections, and all the small details that break in production. I learned to test thoroughly and plan for failure.

### 3. **Balancing Speed with Quality**
The bootcamp's fast pace forced me to find the balance between moving quickly and writing maintainable code. I learned when to refactor, when to use libraries, and when to build from scratch.
